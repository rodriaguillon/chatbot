## Company Foundation
Promtior was founded in May 2023. Its mission is to help organizations transition into a bionic future, where artificial and human intelligence complement and enhance each other.

## Services Offered
Promtior offers technological and organizational consulting services focused on guiding businesses through disruptive environments. By leveraging Generative AI and automation, Promtior enables the development of innovative business models, helping companies maximize emerging opportunities and position themselves as leaders in their industries.
